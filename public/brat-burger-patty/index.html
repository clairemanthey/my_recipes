Brat Burger Patty

Prep Time

7

Cook Time

10

Servings

6

Ingredients

2lbs ground pork or beef

1 TBSP kosher salt

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp ground nutmeg

1/2 tsp ground black pepper

1/2 tsp ground ginger

1/4 tsp coriander

1/4 tsp celery seed

1/4 tsp marjoram

1/4 tsp ground sage

Tools

mixing bowls

frying pan

slotted turner or spatula

instructions

Step 1

Mix ground pork with all seasonings

Step 2

Divide into 1/3lb patties

Step 3

Cook patties on medium heat to your preferred doneness
